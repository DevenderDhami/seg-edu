Our Government Associations

SEG Educational Group Pvt. Ltd. partners with government organizations to implement national skill training programs. Our affiliations include the Ministry of Skill Development, State Skill Missions, and various industry councils.

Through these collaborations, we strive to enhance employability, bridge skill gaps, and support India's workforce transformation.

Impact and Contributions

  • Aligning with national and state skill development policies
  • Training and certifying youth in industry-relevant skills
  • Providing government-recognized certifications
  • Facilitating employment opportunities
  • Contributing to a globally competitive workforce
